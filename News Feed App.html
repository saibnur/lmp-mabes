🤖 Technology
The AI Revolution Is Rewriting the Rules of Every Industry
SL
Sarah Lin
Reuters · 8 min read · 2 hrs ago

The landscape of modern industry is undergoing its most profound transformation since the industrial revolution. Artificial intelligence, once confined to research labs and science fiction, has now permeated every corner of the global economy — from healthcare diagnostics to supply chain logistics, from legal research to creative writing.

"This isn't merely technological change — it's a fundamental restructuring of how human civilization organizes work, knowledge, and value."

Experts warn that the pace of adoption is outstripping society's ability to adapt. Regulatory frameworks remain years behind, educational systems have barely begun to respond, and entire workforce segments face displacement without adequate transition support. Yet proponents argue the net effect will be overwhelmingly positive — unleashing human potential by automating the mundane.
